Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 19, 2026

1. Introduction

ConnectHQ ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise process personal information in connection with our mobile application and services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Authentication Information: Email address, name, and account credentials when you create an account or log in
  • Profile Information: Information you provide in your user profile
  • Event Data: Information related to events, guest lists, and attendance records
  • Device Information: Device identifiers, device type, operating system, and mobile network information
  • Camera Access: Information related to QR code scanning for check-in purposes (QR codes are processed locally on your device)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Authenticate users and prevent fraud
  • Enable QR code scanning and attendee check-in features
  • Send notifications about events and check-ins
  • Generate event certificates and reports
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Respond to user requests and support inquiries

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Event Administrators: If you are an attendee, event organizers may see your check-in status and event information
  • Service Providers: Third parties who assist us in operating our platform (Firebase, Google Cloud)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Firebase and Google Services

ConnectHQ uses Firebase for authentication, data storage, and push notifications. Your data is processed by Google according to their privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy & Security
Note on Encryption: ConnectHQ does not use encryption for purposes subject to export control. All data transmission is done over secure HTTPS connections.

6. Camera and Media Permissions

ConnectHQ requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: To scan QR codes for attendee check-in
  • Photos/Media Library: To save event certificates and gallery images
  • Notifications: To send event updates and check-in confirmations

These permissions are only used for the purposes described above. QR code scanning is performed locally on your device.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of communications
  • Data portability

9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

10. Children's Privacy

ConnectHQ is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the app constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
