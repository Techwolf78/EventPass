How to Delete Your Account

Contact Support to Request Deletion

To delete your account, please send an email to our support team with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include:

  • Your registered email address
  • A brief confirmation that you understand deletion is permanent
  • Any feedback about why you're deleting (optional)

Once we receive your email, we'll send a confirmation link to verify your request. Click the link to confirm, and your account will be scheduled for deletion.

Contact Support

📧 Send Your Deletion Request To:

synergysphere@gryphonacademy.co.in

Subject: Account Deletion Request

We typically respond within 24-48 business hours. Once confirmed, your account will be fully deleted within 30 days.

Data Deletion Timeline

  1. Immediately: Your account access is terminated
  2. Within 7 days: You must confirm the deletion via email (if requested via this form)
  3. Within 30 days: All personal data is permanently deleted from our systems
  4. Within 90 days: Data is purged from all backups

Your Rights

Under data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Receive a copy of your data before deletion
  • Have your data deletion confirmed in writing

If you need any of these requests, please contact us with details.

FAQ

Q: Can I recover my account after deletion?

A: No, account deletion is permanent. Contact support before sending your deletion request if you're reconsidering.

Q: What happens to my event attendance records?

A: Your personal attendance data will be deleted. Event organizers may retain anonymized event statistics, but they won't be able to link records to you.

Q: How long does deletion take?

A: Your account is immediately deactivated after you confirm the verification email. Complete deletion takes up to 30 days.

Q: Will my data be shared with anyone?

A: No, your data will only be used for the deletion process. See our Privacy Policy for more information.

Q: Can I delete just certain data?

A: For specific data deletion requests, please contact synergysphere@gryphonacademy.co.in with details.
