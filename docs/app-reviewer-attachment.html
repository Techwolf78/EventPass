ConnectHQ App Review

Reviewer Access Guide

This document provides test access instructions for ConnectHQ. The app is an event utility for attendee QR passes, event schedules, attendee lists, organizer check-in scanning, and profile/account management.

App Name ConnectHQ EventPass Bundle Identifier com.connecthq.eventpass Support URL https://event-pass-eta.vercel.app/support Marketing URL https://event-pass-eta.vercel.app/marketing

Test Access

Guest / Attendee Access Tab: Guest Full Name: Ajay Email: ajay@gmail.com

Guest access does not require a password. The reviewer should enter the full name and registered email address, accept Terms and Conditions, then tap Continue to Event.

Organizer / Admin Access Tab: Organizer Email: superadmin@test.com Password: 12345678

Organizer access is used to review the admin screens and QR scanner. Please allow camera permission when testing the scanner.

Guest Review Steps

  1. Open the app and keep the Guest tab selected on the Welcome screen.
  2. Enter Full Name: Ajay.
  3. Enter Email Address: ajay@gmail.com.
  4. Accept the Terms and Conditions.
  5. Tap Continue to Event.
  6. Review the QR pass, agenda, attendees, gallery, profile, and delete account flow.

Organizer Review Steps

  1. Open the app and switch to the Organizer tab.
  2. Sign in with superadmin@test.com and 12345678.
  3. Open the scanner screen and allow camera access.
  4. Review guest management, agenda, attendee status, and check-in features.

Review Notes

The app uses Firebase services for authentication and event data. It uses standard HTTPS/Firebase encryption only and does not use custom cryptography or non-exempt encryption.

If any test account cannot be accessed during review, please contact synergysphere@gryphonacademy.co.in.
