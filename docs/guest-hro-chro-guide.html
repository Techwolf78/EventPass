ConnectHQ Event Pass

Quick Guide for Guests

Use this short guide after receiving the event app link by email.

Event App Quick Start

  1. Open the app link Tap the link from the email on your phone. If asked, choose to open it in your browser or app store.
  2. Install and open the app Tap Install or Get, wait for the download, then open ConnectHQ Event Pass.
  3. Continue as Guest and view My Pass On the Welcome screen, keep Guest selected, enter your full name and registered email, then tap Continue to Event. Your QR pass opens first.
  4. View the Attendees tab Tap Attendees from the bottom menu to see the attendee list and search by name, email, or company.
  5. Open the Gallery tab Tap Gallery to view event highlights and photos. Tap any photo to open it larger.
  6. Check your profile details Open Profile to confirm your name, email, role, enrollment, company, and pass ID.
